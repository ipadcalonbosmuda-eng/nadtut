FAQ

What is Plasmatic?

Plasmatic is a comprehensive Web App for deploying tokens, locking tokens & liquidity, managing vesting schedules, and multi-sending on Plasma Network. It provides a simple and transparent platform for all your token management needs.

Where do I deploy and manage tokens?

Inside the Plasmatic Web App at app.plasmatic.to. This website is for information only - all actual token operations happen in the Web App.

Which networks are supported?

Plasmatic is exclusive to Plasma Network. We focus solely on Plasma to provide the best experience and security. Cross-chain support is not planned.

Is contract verification automatic?

Yes. All contracts deployed through the Plasmatic Web App are automatically prepared for verification on the Plasma explorer, ensuring transparency and trust.

Can I export reports for audits?

Yes. You can export CSV reports and share public proof pages for all locks and vesting schedules, making it easy to provide transparency for audits and compliance.

How secure is Plasmatic?

Plasmatic prioritizes security first with on-chain verification, automatic contract verification, and transparent proof systems. All operations are verifiable on the Plasma blockchain.

What fees are involved?

Plasmatic charges minimal fees for operations, with transparent pricing displayed in the Web App. You only pay for the actual blockchain transaction costs plus a small service fee.

Do I need technical knowledge to use Plasmatic?

No. Plasmatic is designed to be user-friendly with a simple interface. You can deploy tokens, set up locks, and manage vesting without any technical expertise.
