Privacy Policy

Last updated: October 2024

1. Introduction

Welcome to Plasmatic ("we," "our," or "us"). We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring the security of your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect information when you use our website (plasmatic.tools) and Web App (app.plasmatic.tools).

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Information You Provide

  • Wallet Address: When you connect your wallet to use our services
  • Transaction Data: Information about tokens, locks, and vesting schedules you create
  • Contact Information: If you contact us for support or feedback

2.2 Automatically Collected Information

  • Usage Data: How you interact with our website and Web App
  • Device Information: Browser type, device type, IP address
  • Cookies: We use essential cookies for website functionality

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information to:

  • Provide and improve our token management services
  • Process your transactions on the Plasma Network
  • Generate public proof pages for transparency
  • Provide customer support and respond to inquiries
  • Ensure security and prevent fraud
  • Comply with legal obligations

4. Information Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information. We may share information only in these circumstances:

  • Blockchain Transparency: Transaction data is publicly visible on the Plasma Network
  • Legal Requirements: When required by law or to protect our rights
  • Service Providers: With trusted third parties who help us operate our services

5. Data Security

We implement appropriate security measures to protect your information:

  • Encryption of sensitive data in transit and at rest
  • Regular security audits and monitoring
  • Access controls and authentication requirements
  • Secure smart contract development practices

6. Blockchain and Decentralization

Important considerations for blockchain-based services:

  • Transactions on Plasma Network are permanent and publicly visible
  • We cannot delete or modify blockchain transactions
  • Your wallet address and transaction history are pseudonymous but traceable
  • Smart contracts operate independently once deployed

7. Your Rights and Choices

You have the right to:

  • Access and review the personal information we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate information
  • Request deletion of your account and associated data (where legally possible)
  • Opt-out of non-essential communications
  • Control cookie preferences in your browser

8. International Data Transfers

Our services may involve data transfers across international borders. We ensure appropriate safeguards are in place to protect your information in accordance with applicable privacy laws.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. We will notify you of significant changes by posting the updated policy on our website and updating the "Last updated" date.

10. Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy or our privacy practices, please contact us:

  • Website: plasmatic.tools
  • Telegram: @plasmatictools
  • Twitter/X: @plasmatictools
